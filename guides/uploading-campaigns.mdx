---
title: "Uploading campaigns"
description: "Import Cobalt Strike, Brute Ratel, or previously exported .redeye campaign files into RedEye."
---

RedEye provides three ways to upload and visualize campaign data. After uploading, your campaign appears in the campaign cards screen and parsing begins automatically.

## Upload methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Cobalt Strike">
    Select the Cobalt Strike option and upload a multi-server or single-server folder.

    RedEye automatically removes files that are not necessary for parsing. To view the full list of removed files, hover over the **File Removed** icon after the upload completes.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Brute Ratel">
    Select the Brute Ratel option and upload your Brute Ratel campaign folder.

    RedEye parses and visualizes the campaign data automatically.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title=".redeye File">
    Upload a database file ending in `.redeye` or `.sqlite`.

    These files are typically campaigns that were previously exported from RedEye. See [Exporting campaigns](/guides/exporting-campaigns) for details on how to produce these files.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Parsing status

After a campaign is uploaded, RedEye parses the data before it is available for exploration. The campaign card shows the current parsing status:

| Status | Description |
|---|---|
| `PARSING_NOT_STARTED` | Upload complete, parsing has not yet begun |
| `PARSING_QUEUED` | Campaign is queued and waiting to be parsed |
| `PARSING_IN_PROGRESS` | RedEye is actively parsing the campaign data |
| `PARSING_COMPLETED` | Parsing is finished; the campaign is ready to open |
| `PARSING_FAILURE` | An error occurred during parsing |

<Note>
  If a campaign shows `PARSING_FAILURE`, verify that the uploaded folder or file matches the selected parser type.
</Note>

## Managing campaigns

Once uploaded, campaigns appear as cards on the campaign screen. To rename, delete, or export a campaign, click the **More** icon on the campaign card.

You can filter through the list of campaigns by typing in the input field next to the **Add a campaign** button.
